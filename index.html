Why Listo?

Tasks and Notes Together

Most apps make you choose: task manager OR note-taker. Listo gives you both. Create a task with a deadline, or just capture a quick thought. It's all in one place.

Organize Your Way

Projects for big-picture thinking. Tags with custom colors for the details. Your brain doesn't work in rigid categories. Neither should your productivity app.

Your Data Stays Yours

Everything lives on your device. No cloud sync, no data collection, no servers. We can't see your tasks because they never leave your phone. Privacy by design, not by promise.

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Smart Inbox

See what needs attention: overdue tasks, today's agenda, and recent notes, all in one view.

Recurring Tasks

Set it once, forget about it. Daily standup? Weekly review? Listo handles the repetition.

Custom Tags

Create unlimited tags with your own colors. Tag the same task as "urgent," "client work," and "email" if that's how you think.

Flexible Projects

Group related tasks and notes together. Move things between projects freely, or keep them standalone.

Archive Everything

Finished projects cluttering your view? Archive them. They're still there if you need them, but out of your way when you don't.

Light & Dark Mode

Looks great whether you're planning your day or winding down at night.
