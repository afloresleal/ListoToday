Privacy Policy for Burn

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

Burn ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our fitness tracking application handles your information.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Workout Records: The workouts you log (type, duration, date)
  • Notification Preferences: The time you choose for daily reminders
  • App Settings: Your preferences within the app

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version (for app functionality only)
  • App Usage: Basic app performance and crash data to improve the app

How We Store Your Data

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not:

  • Upload your workouts or activity data to our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Back up your data to cloud services
  • Track your activities outside the app
  • Use AI APIs or external services to generate messages

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

About Burnie (Your Coach)

All coaching messages are pre-written and stored within the app. Burnie's "personality" runs entirely on your deviceâ€”no external AI services, no data sent anywhere. Your workout data is never transmitted to generate messages or notifications.

Permissions We Request

Notifications

  • Purpose: To send you daily reminders at your chosen time (Burnie calling you out)
  • Control: You can grant or deny this permission in iOS Settings
  • Data: Notification content (Burnie's messages) is generated locally on your device
  • Timing: You choose when Burnie reminds you; we don't decide for you

No Other Permissions Required

Burn does not request access to:

  • Your contacts
  • Your location
  • Your camera or photos
  • Your microphone
  • Your calendar
  • Your health data (HealthKit)
  • Any other device features

Data Security

  • All data is stored securely using Apple's built-in encryption
  • Data is protected by your device's passcode/biometric authentication
  • We use industry-standard security practices in our code
  • Notification scheduling is handled by iOS, not external servers

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it
  • You can delete individual workouts at any time
  • The app includes a "Clear All Data" option for complete deletion
  • Deleting the app will permanently remove all your data from your device
  • Scheduled notifications are automatically removed when you delete the app
  • We cannot recover your data once it's deleted

Data Export

  • You can export your workout history as Markdown (.md) files
  • Exported files are readable in apps like Notion, Obsidian, or any text editor
  • Exports are generated on-device and saved to your Files app
  • You control where exported files are saved and who can access them

Children's Privacy

Burn does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general fitness tracking and does not require age verification.

Third-Party Services

Burn does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party tracking tools
  • Social media integrations
  • AI APIs or external message generation services
  • Cloud sync services

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • An update to the "Last Updated" date
  • An in-app notification (for major changes)
  • An App Store update description

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Export your data as Markdown files
  • Delete individual workouts or all data at any time
  • Control notification permissions and timing
  • Use the app completely offline

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with anyone. Your workout data is yours alone.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how Burn handles your data, please contact us at:

Email: hello@burnapp.fitness

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles

Summary

In plain English:

  • We don't collect your data
  • Everything stays on your device
  • Notifications are scheduled locally (no server tracking)
  • No AI APIs or external services
  • No analytics, no ads, no tracking
  • Delete the app = delete your data
  • We can't see your workouts or streaks
  • Burnie's sass is 100% offline

This privacy policy is effective as of January 28, 2026, and applies to all users of Burn.
