Privacy Policy for Listo

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

Introduction

Listo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our task management application handles your information.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Tasks and Projects: The tasks, projects, notes, and tags you create within the app
  • Reminder Settings: Times and dates you set for task reminders
  • App Settings: Your preferences and settings within the app

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version (for app functionality only)
  • App Usage: Basic app performance and crash data to improve the app

How We Store Your Data

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not:

  • Upload your tasks, notes, or projects to our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Back up your data to cloud services
  • Track your activities outside the app

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Permissions We Request

Notifications

  • Purpose: To remind you about tasks at scheduled times
  • Control: You can grant or deny this permission in iOS Settings
  • Data: Notification content (task titles) is processed locally on your device

No Other Permissions Required

Listo does not request access to:

  • Your contacts
  • Your location
  • Your camera or photos
  • Your microphone
  • Any other device features

Data Security

  • All data is stored securely using Apple's built-in encryption
  • Data is protected by your device's passcode/biometric authentication
  • We use industry-standard security practices in our code

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it
  • You can delete individual tasks, projects, or notes at any time
  • Deleting the app will permanently remove all your data from your device
  • We cannot recover your data once it's deleted

Children's Privacy

Listo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general task management and does not require age verification.

Third-Party Services

Listo does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party tracking tools
  • Social media integrations

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • An update to the "Last Updated" date
  • An in-app notification (for major changes)
  • An App Store update description

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Export your data (if you enable this feature)
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Control notification permissions

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with anyone. Your data is yours alone.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how Listo handles your data, please contact us at:

Email: hola@listo.today
Website: https://listo.today

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles

Summary

In plain English:

  • We don't collect your data
  • Everything stays on your device
  • We don't use analytics or ads
  • Delete the app = delete your data
  • We can't see what you're working on

This privacy policy is effective as of December 4, 2025, and applies to all users of Listo.
